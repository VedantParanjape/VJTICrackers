About us

A web app for centralisation of patients' medical history, irrespective of his/her location, hospital visited, doctors consulted, making it easier than ever to access his/her medical records and get a clear picture of the entire health history. Multiple doctors can register with this webapp and after getting permission from a user, a particular doctor can access the medical history of the patient. Further, risk assessment based on patient history and location will be implemented, using Machine Learning.
